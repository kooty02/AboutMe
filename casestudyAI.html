AUTISM
IMPROVISED
UX UI REDESIGN CASE STUDY

Responsible for choosing a non-profit organization and executing a high-fidelity responsive web design solution through a clickable prototype.

MY ROLE

Brand Design / Information Architecture / Responsive Web Design

TIMELINE

08/08/19 - 08/28/19 (3 weeks)

TEAM

Tae Yun Koo / Jonathan Kaplan / Khadijah Khan

ABOUT

Autism Improvised was designed to achieve a safe, fun, and beneficial experience for children and young adults living with Autism.

PROBLEM

Convey organization ideals and benefits clear and easy, visually sharing the story of the organization's mission.

RESEARCH

The research phase consisted of an informational interview with the stakeholders of Autism Improvised, surveys through google forms, and 1 on 1 interviews. Interviews were conducted with people that had familiarity Autism, or worked with non-profits either through volunteering or as a professional. Ideally we would have liked to have a larger sample size and time to restructure questions to be more specific to the participant being interviewed.
